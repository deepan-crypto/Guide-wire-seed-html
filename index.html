AI-Powered Parametric Income Protection

Replacing the outdated, manual P&C claims process with an autonomous safety net that protects a gig worker's earning capacity and pays out instantly.

1. The Problem Statement

Gig workers form the backbone of quick-commerce, yet they are the most financially vulnerable. During extreme conditions (heatwaves, floods, pollution), they face a tough decision: work in unsafe conditions OR lose their daily income.

Traditional insurance fails them. This leads to extreme income instability and severe safety risks.

2. The Solution: RiskWire

RiskWire is an AI-powered, parametric micro-insurance product built natively on the Guidewire Cloud Platform (GWCP) designed for instant support.

No paperwork, no delays, no manual intervention. Automatically triggers payouts without manual claims when riders are forced offline.

Target Persona & The Guidewire Advantage

Target: Quick-Commerce

Our primary focus is on hyper-local delivery partners (Platforms: Swiggy, Zomato). They are highly vulnerable to localized disruptions.

  • Real-time operational data is available.
  • Lost income is easy to quantify.
  • Disruptions are hyper‑local and instantly visible.

Why GWCP?

Traditional insurance cannot scale to the gig economy because of high monthly premiums and slow manual claims. We chose GWCP because it provides enterprise primitives:

  • Dynamic Micro-Premiums: SaaS-style weekly coverage tiers (Basic, Standard, Pro).
  • Scalable Frontend: Accessible, mobile-first experience for riders in high-motion environments.

🔥 The Innovation Edge (Our USPs)

A. Dynamic 3-Tier Business Model

Flexible subscription model (Basic, Standard, Pro). Premiums are adjusted using an AI-based Risk Multiplier. Higher risk zones result in a slightly higher premium, making it affordable and scalable.

B. AI Voice-First Multilingual Assistant

Powered by an In-Context RAG Assistant (Gemini), Vani AI feeds policy Q&A directly to the user in their native language.

C. Income-Aware Dual Validation

  • External Disruption: Rain > 50mm/hr, Heat > 42°C, AQI > 400.
  • Platform Status: Aggregator API verifies if order volume dropped or zone is offline.

D. Growth & Security Ecosystem

Referral system for mass adoption coupled with strong authentication & identity validation, and spam call detection.

📱 The Frontend: Jutro Digital Platform

We leverage Guidewire’s Jutro framework to build accessible, React-based interfaces tailored for two distinct users: the gig worker in high-motion environments, and the insurer monitoring macro-risks.

A. The Rider Mobile App

  • Step 1: Frictionless Onboarding & KYC: Instant identity verification via DigiLocker/Aadhaar to prevent "ghost rider" accounts. Riders select their specific Q-commerce micro-zone.
  • Step 2: Dynamic Subscription: The UI displays Basic, Standard, and Pro weekly plans. Prices dynamically fetch the AI-adjusted premium in real-time.
  • Step 3: AI Voice Hub: A persistent microphone allows riders to ask complex policy questions (e.g., "If it rains tomorrow, how much will I get paid?") and receive instant translated voice replies.
  • Step 4: Zero-Touch Claim Tracker: A visual pipeline updating automatically: Monitoring Weather ➔ Trigger Met ➔ Validating Shift Status ➔ Payout Sent.

B. The Admin / Insurer Web Dashboard

  • Risk Heatmaps: Built using Jutro components and Recharts, fed by our Pandas ETL pipeline to show real-time "Insured vs. Uninsured" density maps across city zones.
  • Fraud Audit Log: Displays real-time blocked transactions flagged by our Anti-Spoofing AI, detailing the exact reason (e.g., "VPN Datacenter IP Detected" or "Mock Location Flagged").

🚨 Crisis Management Protocols

Server-Side: The Market Crash Protocol

If our Python ML Oracle detects a catastrophic macro-drop in aggregator order volumes (e.g., >70% drop), it triggers the Dynamic Solvency Protocol inside Guidewire.

  • Macro-API Monitoring: We monitor the macro health of the platforms, not individual behaviors.
  • Pro-Tier Lock & Liquidity Shift: GWCP locks new 'Pro'/'Standard' tier policies. Active liquidity shifts to subsidize the 'Basic' survival tier.
  • The Zero-Delay Guarantee: We guarantee 100% instant execution of the Basic tier for minimum-wage survival payouts immediately.

Client-Side: Zero-Trust Anti-Spoofing

Standard GPS is mathematically compromised. RiskWire protects Guidewire ClaimCenter using a Multi-Layered Spatial ML & Hardware Verification Framework.

  • Hardware-Backed Verification: Google Play Integrity API blocks emulators and rooted devices.
  • Defeating OS Mocking: Catching developer settings via Location.isMock() and "Dead Metadata" traps (e.g., exact 0.0000 speeds).
  • Defeating VPN Proxies: ASN Checks (Telecom vs Datacenter) and Speed of Light (RTT Latency) temporal mismatch blocks.
  • BSSID Clustering: Exposing syndicates sharing the exact same physical Wi-Fi router MAC address.
